Train Dynamics Calulator

The purpose of this program is to give insight into dynamics of braking annd acceleration for different trians. Currently this will be limited to just Victorian train but later may be applied more generally.

This should be used as a tool but its accuracy cannot be gauranteed as there are many limitations with the data.

Some ntoes, on limitations:

Vline - Verp Note: Verp takes acceleration as a*0.9 (maybe for safety?). Vline uses constant braking rates which is probably inaccurate especially for lower speeds.

EMU - these calculations use Xtrap for acceleration and comeng for braking, acceleration rate is probably overestimated for these trains.
